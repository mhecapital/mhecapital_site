About Us

MHE Capital is dedicated to the study of financial markets through a rational and research-based approach. Our work blends probabilistic thinking, macro-financial analysis and mathematical modeling to interpret market behavior with clarity.

We focus on market structure, behavioral patterns and inter-market dynamics to develop a deeper understanding of price movements and long-term economic cycles.

Our Research Areas

Our expertise spans several core pillars for understanding modern financial markets.

Macroeconomics & Cycles

Analysis of economic cycles, sentiment, market regimes, and structural trends.

Market Modeling & Probability Theory

Research on stochastic modeling, probability theory and mathematical structures behind market dynamics.

Investor Behavior

Study of cognitive biases, market psychology, positioning, and derivative data.

Sector & Inter-Market Analysis

Research on correlations between sectors, asset classes, and macro drivers.
